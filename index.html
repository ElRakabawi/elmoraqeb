Self-monitoring tool for Thanaweya a'mma students

The story behind

As a student going to Thanaweya A'mma this year, i found that i will have a lot

of time over the year, without a single way of managing and monitoring it.

I Used the Self-monitoring technique with stuff outside the school focus,

and it's very effecient.

In Egypt, most Thanaweya A'mma students takes courses, therefor quizes, a

lot of it, quizes means data, data means representations, charts!

I made a private tool at first, then i thought it will be helpful

if it was availabe for all other Thanaweya A'mma students.


note: You can for sure use this tool for managing your time, and

and tracking levels-over-time of almost anything, from sporting

to Studyig. anything contains data can be represnted, your imagination(or your data)

is the only the limit.
